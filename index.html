Dungeon Wizard

A souls-like/rogue-lite centered around a 3rd person combat system heavily focused on magic attacks, spells and corresponding elemental effects like burning (Dot), frost (debuff), storm (stagger and damage amplifying), etc.

Enter a dungeon with a variety of different enemies and mysteries hidden within the deepest parts.
Upgrade your arsenal of magic attacks and spells by unlocking them through different assignments.
During the time in the dungeon, defeating different boss monsters will grand the favor of the gods until your undeniable death.

Catch

Fast pace, adrenaline inducing multiplayer online fun for everyone (except the slow players).
Try to escape the catcher by using the physics-based parkour like movement to climb, jump or slide around the map. If you happen to be caught and take over the catcher position another movement option is added to your arsenal, use the grabbling gun to maneuver the map even faster to surprise your target.
